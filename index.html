Waffles

A sinfully delicious, crisp, raised cake, baked in a waffle iron with a honeycomb-like indentations on its surface.

Ingredients:

  • 250g All-purpose flour
  • 50g Sugar
  • 1/2tsp Salt
  • 4 Large eggs
  • 2tsp Baking powder
  • 355mL Whole milk
  • 120mL Buttermilk
  • 113g Melted butter
  • Maple syrup, honey, berries, or ice cream for serving as desired.

Procedure:

  • Step 1- Preheat the waffle iron
  • Step 2- Sieve and mix together the dry ingredients(baking powder, flour, sugar and salt) into a large bowl and set aside
  • Step 3- In a separate bowl, gently whisk the eggs, milk, and Buttermilk
  • Step 4- Add the above mixture into the dry mixture and mix until just combined
  • Step 5- Pour the melted butter into the batter and whisk
  • Step 6- Pour about half a cup of the batter into the waffle iron and spread unti it covers the entirety of the iron
  • Step 7- Close the waffle-iron and cook for 3 minutes or until crisp and golden-brown on the outside
  • Step 8- Set the waffles onto a plate and top with berries, ice-cream, nutella, or honey, as per your choice

Enjoy~
